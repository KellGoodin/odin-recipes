Description

A simple turkey & cheese panini recipe with the zing of a korean sauce. For those of you who do not like spicy things, feel free to substitue with cranberry sauce.
This is a great sandwhich for a fast lunch or even a simple dinner! Just make sure to have a little something like fruit or chips as a side dish too.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough Bread (two slices)
  • Thin-sliced deli turkey - preferably a spicy buffalo, peppered, or oven roasted style
  • Havarti cheese (1-2 slices depending on preference and bread size)
  • A Korean Gochujang sauce of your choice or cranberry sauce of your choicee

Steps

  1. Heat panini pan over stove on medium-high heat, while heating up braise outer faces of the bread with a light coating of olive oil. Use a brush if able.
  2. Add several slices of turkey, one to two slices of havarti, and add sauce to taste.
  3. Once the pan is hot and ready, add the sandwhich and put the press on top.
  4. Let it sit for about 1 minute to 1 minute and half before flipping sides. Do a little more than half that time for the other side.
  5. Plate and you're all done!
